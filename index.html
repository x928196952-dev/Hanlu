Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

We spent a year talking to Chinese learners about what actually works. Here's what made the cut.

声

Tone training AI

Record yourself, see your pitch curve in real time, get instant feedback. Fix tone mistakes before they calcify into habits.

字

Character system

Learn stroke order visually, understand radicals as building blocks, and use spaced repetition to retain 3,000+ characters.

对

AI conversation partner

Chat or voice practice any time. Our AI adjusts difficulty, explains errors in plain English, and never makes you feel stupid.

考

HSK exam prep

Full mock exams for HSK 1–6 with timed sections, instant scoring, and a weakness report that tells you exactly what to review.

化

Cultural context

Why do you never give a clock as a gift? What does it mean when someone says "have you eaten yet?" Language without culture is half-learned.

路

Clear learning path

No more wondering what to study next. A 12-month curriculum built by linguists — from your first pinyin to your first real conversation.
